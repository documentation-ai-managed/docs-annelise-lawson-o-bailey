## Overview

Create and organize new projects in your dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Projects">
    Navigate to the Projects tab on the main sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Project">
    Press the "Add Project" button in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Details">
    Enter your project name and a short description, then click Save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Done

Your new project will appear in the list.

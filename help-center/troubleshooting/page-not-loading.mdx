---
title: Project Page Not Loading
description: How to solve issues where the project page won't load.
---

## Symptoms

- Project page stays blank
- Error: "Failed to load content"

## Causes

- Slow internet
- Browser cache issues

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh browser">
    ````
    Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac) to force refresh.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Go to browser settings, clear cache and cookies, then reload the page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

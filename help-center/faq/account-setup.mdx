## Steps

1. Visit `https://portal.anneliselawsonobailey.com/signup`
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Click the confirmation link in your email
4. Log in to your new dashboard

## Tips

- Use a strong, unique password
- If you don't see the confirmation email, check spam folder

## Need help?
Email support@anneliselawsonobailey.com if you have issues.
